This page is to commemorate the local teams here in Utah. We have five college teams here they are BYU, University of Utah, Utah State, Utah Valley University, and Westminster College. Below is further information you can find regarding these teams we also have teams in high school, jr high, youth leagues, and a mens league. Utahlax is a website designated for lacrosse locally for the highschool,youth, and mens leagues it will help you find schedules,rosters, news,events, and resources. Utahlax is a chapter of Uslacrosse they are the sports national governing body they provide further structure,leadership, and resources for lacrosse.
